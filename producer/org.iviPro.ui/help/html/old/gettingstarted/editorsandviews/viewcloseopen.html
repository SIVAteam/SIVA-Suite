Closing and Reopening of Views


Views can be closed with a click on the close-cross right next to the view name.

In order to open a view, the button in the lower left corner "Show View as a fast view" is clicked. In the opening window, all views of the SIVA Producer can be found in the folder "Other".



Here, it is possible to mark all views that shall be opened. A click on "OK" opens the views which can than be arranged freely in the working area.
