Closing and restoring views


Views can be closed by clicking onto the closing-cross on the right next to the name of the view.

To open a view you press the button "Show View as a fast view" in the right corner below. If you click onto "Other" or you double click onto , a new window opens. In the file "Other" all the views of the SIVA Producers are listed.





Here it is possible to mark all of the views that should be opened. By clicking onto "OK" the views will open and after that can be ordered freely in the working space.
