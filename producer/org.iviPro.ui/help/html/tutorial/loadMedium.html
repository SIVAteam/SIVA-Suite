Adding media files

After creating the project you can load media that are shown in the media repository. Click on the 'load media'-button in the toolbar ('+'-button). The 'open'-window is opened by choosing a file that you want to load.

Subsequent types of media can be chosen:

  • video
  • audio
  • picture
  • pdf
  • text

Please load for the tutorial two video files and one picture file.
After the loading your media repository have to look like in the picture below.

Next you have to convert the videos into scenes or you cut it to integrate them into the scene graph, so that can be a part of the interactive video.

You can do this on two different ways by right click on the video and after that click on:
  • You want that the complete video is a scene in your interactive video: create scene over the full length of the movie.
  • You want that only a part of the video is a scene in your interactive video: edit scene
If you prefer the first option the complete video is a scene in you scene repository.
If you choose the second option, the cutting of a video is explain in the next menu item.
Important for this tutorial is, that you add three scenes in the scene repository.
Please click for the editing of the scenes on the next menu item 'cutting a video' in the help index on the left side or on the link below. Cutting a video

Notice: A video have to be converted into a scene, so you can integrate it into the interactive video.

Notice: At least one video have to be loaded to create an interactive video!
