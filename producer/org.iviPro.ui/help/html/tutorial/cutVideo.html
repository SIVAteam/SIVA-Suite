Cutting videos

You have chosen that you don't want to add the whole video as a scene to the interactive video, but only a segment. For that click with the right mouse button on your video, so that the context menu is opened. After that click on the 'edit scene'-button to open the scene editor.

In the scene editor you can move the yellow beginning bar and the red end bar within the timeline to cut your video. Or you limit the scene by the input of the start and end time in the 'time input'-section.

Furthermore you have the option to name the title of the scene in the section below or to assign key words.

Finally you click on the 'save'-button to finish the cutting and to saving the scene in the scene repository.
