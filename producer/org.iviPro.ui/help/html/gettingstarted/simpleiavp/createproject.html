Creating an Interactive Video Project

To create an interactive video project, a click on the "New Project" button opens the following dialogue:



After selecting the storage location with a click on the browse button and navigating to the desired folder in the explorer, a project name can be set. Clicking on "Next" leads to the player settings dialogue:



In this dialogue, the player resolution, the annotation bar visibility, the annotation bar behavior, the sidebar sizes, the player colors, the player functions, and the video settings can be set. Clicking "Finish" starts the creation of the interactive video.
